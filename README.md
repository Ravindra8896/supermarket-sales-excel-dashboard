# supermarket-sales-excel-dashboard
Interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 1000+ supermarket sales transactions to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance insights.
📊 Supermarket Sales Dashboard (Excel)
🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes 1000+ supermarket transactional records using Microsoft Excel to uncover business insights related to revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and payment preferences. All 3 branches are within 4% revenue of each other 

The objective was to transform raw sales data into an interactive and business-driven dashboard.

🛠 Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

KPI Cards

Slicers (Interactive Filters)

Data Cleaning & Formatting

Derived Time Features (Month, Day, Hour)

Data Visualization & Dashboard Design

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

💰 Total Revenue: $322,966.75

📦 Total Orders: 1000

⭐ Average Customer Rating: 6.97

🛒 Average Order Quantity: 5.51

📊 Dashboard Insights

🥇 Food & Beverages is the highest revenue-generating product category.

👩 Female customers contribute approximately 57.1% of total sales.

💳 E-wallet and Cash is the most preferred payment method [Ewallet=345, Cash=344]...

📅 January recorded the highest monthly revenue.

🏬 Giza branch leads in revenue performance.🎯 Business Recommendations

Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.
